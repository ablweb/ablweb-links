About Me

I am Abel, I live in Brussels, Belgium, and am currently enrolled in a Bachelor's in Computer Science at ULB (Université libre de Bruxelles). I am in my second year of the program and third year at university. What interests me most is automation. When I see manufacturing processes, I ask how tasks can be automated and protocols optimized to increase value. Furthermore, some of my other interests include learning about AI and data science, following the world of space exploration, and solving algorithmic problems. Above all, my work focuses on achieving great design: simple, readable, maintainable, and scalable, which is reflected in the tools I use. I mainly work with C, Go, and C++, but I also use Python in AI contexts (PyTorch, TensorFlow, Pandas, ...) and for scripting.
